# What is Economics?
## Scarcity and the Factors of Production
### Economics: The study of how people seek to satisfy their needs and wants by making choices
### Needs and wants: Two of the biggest factors in shaping the decisions everyone make on a daily basis

<!-- ## Scarcity and it's Role in Economics -->
### Scarcity: people are faced with a limited quantity of goods and services to meet their unlimited wants

## What resources are needed to create goods and services?

![factors of productions](Images/factors_of_production.png "Factors of Production")

 ### 1. Land: Natural resource used to produce goods/services
 ### 2. Labor: Effort towards a task that you are paid for
 ### 3. Capital: Man-made resources that are used to produce other goods and services

 ### These three resources are called the factors of production.

 ### Entrepreneurs: are the individuals responsible for pulling together the factors of production(land, labor, capital) and create the goals and services we enjoy today
